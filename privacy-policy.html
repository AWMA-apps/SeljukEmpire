Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 24, 2025

Tareekh Al-Dawla Al-Saljuqiyya (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. However, the App uses Firebase Realtime Database and Firebase Storage to store and deliver content (text and images). These services may collect non-identifiable technical data such as device information, IP addresses, and crash reports, which are processed by Firebase in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Information

Any data collected by Firebase is used only to:

  • Deliver and display the app’s content.
  • Maintain and improve the app’s performance.
  • Ensure secure and reliable operation of Firebase services.
We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app relies on Firebase services. You can learn more about Firebase’s privacy practices here: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data stored in the app. Firebase provides industry-standard security for storing and transferring data.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page with an updated “Effective Date.”
